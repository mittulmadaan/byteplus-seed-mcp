# Safety & responsible use

Audio generation — especially voice cloning — carries real-world risk. Apply these before
submitting.

## Voice cloning consent

- Only clone a voice the user is **authorised** to use: their own voice, a voice with explicit
  permission, or a properly licensed asset.
- Do **not** clone a real, identifiable person (public figures, celebrities, someone's private
  recording) to make them appear to say things they didn't, especially for deception,
  impersonation, fraud, or harassment.
- If a request looks like non-consensual impersonation, decline and explain why; offer a
  preset voice instead.

## Content

- No audio intended to defraud, scam, or impersonate for financial/political gain.
- No harassing, threatening, or hateful content.
- Be cautious with content that could be mistaken for a real emergency announcement, official
  statement, or authority figure.

## Copyright

- Don't reproduce copyrighted scripts, song lyrics, or performances without rights.
- Reference audio should be material the user has the rights to.

## Disclosure

- For synthetic voices presented to third parties, encourage the user to disclose that the
  audio is AI-generated where appropriate or legally required.

When in doubt, ask the user to confirm they have the rights/consent, and prefer preset voices
over cloning.
